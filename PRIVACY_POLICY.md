## 1. Overview

Dayring is a productivity app for planning tasks, tracking focus sessions, and viewing progress. This policy explains what data Dayring handles, how it is used, and your choices.

## 2. Data Dayring Handles

Dayring may handle the following categories of data:
- Task and planning data you create in the app (for example: task titles, notes, categories, schedule times, checklist items, completion status, focus session records).
- Settings and preferences (for example: theme, onboarding choices, calendar integration preferences, reminder preferences).
- Subscription status data needed to unlock premium features.
- Optional calendar event data if you grant Calendar permission.
- Optional notification settings if you grant notification permission.

## 3. How Data Is Used

Dayring uses data to:
- Provide core app functionality.
- Save your plans and progress.
- Show reminders and schedule-related features.
- Enable premium features and restore purchases.
- Improve reliability and resolve support issues.

## 4. Permissions

Dayring may request:
- Calendar access: used to show your calendar events alongside your plan.
- Notification access: used to send reminders for your scheduled tasks.

You can change permission choices at any time in iOS Settings.

## 5. Data Storage and Sync

- Local data: Dayring stores your app data on your device.
- Optional cloud sync: premium users may use iCloud/CloudKit sync for task data across devices.
- Widgets: Dayring may store a limited shared snapshot so widgets can display current progress and upcoming tasks.

## 6. Purchases and Subscription Processing

Dayring uses Apple's in-app purchase system and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and entitlements.

Apple handles payment information. Dayring does not directly receive your full payment card details.

## 7. Third-Party Services

Dayring may rely on:
- Apple services (App Store, iCloud/CloudKit, StoreKit, notifications, calendar frameworks).
- RevenueCat (subscription and entitlement management).

Please review those providers' privacy terms as well.

## 8. Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal data.

Data is shared only as needed to provide app functionality (for example, Apple and RevenueCat services described above).

## 9. Data Retention

Dayring keeps data until you delete it, remove the app, or otherwise clear data.

You can export or import your data in the app and manage stored content directly from Dayring.

## 10. Your Choices

You can:
- Disable Calendar or Notifications permissions in iOS Settings.
- Delete app data by removing content in the app or uninstalling the app.
- Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Dayring is not directed to children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 12. International Processing

Depending on the Apple or third-party services you use, data may be processed in countries other than your own.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
